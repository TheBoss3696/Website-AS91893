Rentable Chalets

We offer a total of 3 chalets!

All three chalets are on the mountain, so you can ski to and from the chalets!!

Kakapo

  • Largest of the 3 chalets best for a honeymoon!

  • Large kitchen, dining and living area with Great amenities

Kereru

  • Great for large groups/families wanting a chalet to themselves

  • Limits on adult/child number ensure comfortable stay

Pukeko

  • Great option for a family with Separate living area

  • 2 bedrooms which are Perfect for families with 2-4 children
